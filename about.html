I built the thing
I wished existed.

Hi, I'm Ewelina.

When I was 23, I sat on my bedroom floor at 2am, googling "what should I do with my life" for the hundredth time. I'd taken every personality test. I'd read the career books. I'd even tried a life coach. And I still felt completely lost.

The problem wasn't that I didn't have options. It was that nothing connected the dots between who I was and what I should actually do about it. Every tool gave me a label. None gave me a direction.

So I built the thing I wished existed.

Pathlit is a career self-discovery platform that maps your personality, strengths, values, and career fit into one blueprint — then gives you specific paths forward. Not labels. Not vague advice. Real direction you can act on today.

I built it for the person I was at 23. And for every person who's lying awake right now, wondering if they'll ever figure it out.

(Spoiler: you will. You just need the right mirror.)
